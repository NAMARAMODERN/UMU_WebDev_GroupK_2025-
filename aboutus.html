About Us

Welcome to the UMU Gaming Zone — the ultimate hub for gamers at Uganda Martyrs University. We provide an exciting, vibrant and safe environment where students can enjoy console gaming, tournaments, virtual challenges and e-sports competitions. Our mission is to build a strong gaming community by offering quality equipment, competitive events and a fun social space for all gamers.
